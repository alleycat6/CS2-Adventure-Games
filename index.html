CS2 Adventure Game Showcase

Welcome! This block in CS2, students designed and implemented their own text-based adventure games (like Zork!). On this website, you can check out the code and try playing the games yourself! Here are the games:

Ameya Aria Avery Dash Elina Jade Jayden Joseph Kira Lionel Milan Oliver Soeren Sylvia Theo

Some of the games work differently, and some may explain the rules at the beginning. But as a basic cheat sheet, here are some of the most common commands, which everyone implemented in the first version of their game:

  • Type a direction such as "n" or "north" to move in that direction (or say "go" or "move")
  • Type "l" or "look" to look around the room you’re currently in
  • Type "take itemname" to pick up an item currently in the room and put it in your inventory
  • Type "drop itemname" to drop an item currently in your inventory back into the current room
  • Type "examine itemname" to look at an item (may not work for items not in your inventory)
  • Type "score" to see your current score
  • Type "q" or "quit" to end the game

You can also click the “Code” button (with the pencil icon next to it) to see the actual code! (Doing that will end your current game, though.)

Have fun!!
